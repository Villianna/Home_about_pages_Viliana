About me...

I'm an artist and graphic designer who was born and raised in Bulgaria.Drawing and reading were my daily obsession in childhood and I dreamed to become an artist,illustrator and fashion designer who lives in USA someday.Ironically,I chose different path later in life which didn’t bring much happiness and fullfilement to me but it brought me to the path which was origially intended for me. I chose not to pursue Art as a career and I graduated with kinesiology degree from bulgarian university instead.Life brought me back to Art later in life after I won the Green card lottary and moved to San Francisco in 2009.I took my first drawing lesson at City College of San Francisco as an escapism from the boredom, emptiness, disappointments ,even the pain which I felt at this point in my life but soon I felt the most as myself and the child I was with the only diiference that this time I had a lot to say and express.My artwork is representation of my dreams,thoughts,feelings,longings and the balance which I seek in myself, my own life and the world surrounding me.I believe it's also the representation of my childhood dreams facing real life and my destiny.
